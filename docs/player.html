Interactive ROM Player

This demo showcases rom-scout's ability to identify ROMs and fetch metadata, integrated with EmulatorJS for in-browser gameplay. Click any ROM card to play. For ZIP files, the library automatically extracts the contents.

Note: Metadata is fetched from hasheous.org. Drag and drop or click the "+" card to add your own ROMs!
